Monowork Trading Competition

Dear Monowork Users: We welcome you to participate in the “Monowork Trading Competition”. You will have an opportunity to share a 300,000 USDT prize pool and receive an award in BTC, ETH or USDT.

Terms and Conditions

  • The event apply both for existing and new users.
  • The platform takes a snapshot of the user’s account assets every day at 00:00 as a base, and calculates the change in the overall profit rate of account assets at 23:59 that day as the profit rate of the day.
  • In this competition, only spot orders will be taken into account when calculating the trading volume. Volume and profit from margin orders are not taken into account.
  • Rewards will be distributed within two days after the campaign ends.
  • Our Exchange reserve the right to the final explanation of the campaign.
  • Our Exchange reserve the right to disqualify any participants showing signs of fraudulent behavior or users that violate the rules of self-dealing, wash trading, and use multiple accounts..
